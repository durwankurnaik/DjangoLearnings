U Mumba.

Played a total of 131 matches in total with 81 won, 42 loss and 8 Draws. They have the highest win % of 61.83%, lowest loss percentage of 32.06% and even the lowest tie ratio of about 6.10%. Anup kumar definitely played a great role in the success of U Mumba

Here comes the next giants.
Patna Pirates.

They are definitely one of the best team in Pro Kabaddi league. They won total of 3 titles out of 7. They are the best team when it comes to winnig championship. Major reason being their star raider Pardeep Narwal. Sandeep Narwal ans Rahul Kumal also played great roles in previous seasons of Patna, but Pardeep Narwal is the main reason for their success

No matter what kind of players they have, he always finds a way to make it the best team
